About Us

Founded in 2010, Albert Park Accounting Services has been providing expert financial guidance to businesses across Australia. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make professional accounting services accessible to businesses of all sizes. Over the years, we've grown to become a trusted partner for hundreds of successful enterprises.

Our mission is to empower businesses through financial clarity and strategic guidance. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering personalized solutions that drive growth. Our core values of integrity, excellence, and innovation guide everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive accounting services including tax planning and preparation, bookkeeping, financial analysis, business advisory, and compliance services. Our solutions are tailored to meet your specific business needs.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the scope and complexity of services required. We offer flexible packages starting from $250/month for basic bookkeeping. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your needs.

How often will we meet to discuss my finances?

We schedule regular quarterly reviews with all clients, but we're available for consultation whenever needed. Our team provides monthly reports and updates, and we can arrange additional meetings based on your requirements.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes, we work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises. Our services are scalable and can be customized to match your business growth stage and requirements.

What software do you use?

We work with all major accounting software including Xero, MYOB, and QuickBooks. We can adapt to your existing systems or recommend the best solution for your business needs.
